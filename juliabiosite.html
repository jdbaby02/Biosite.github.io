ABOUT JULIA

I am 28 years old, born in the month of August in Nebraska. I was raised by my mother, Jen and currently live in Council Bluffs. I have two older siblings, Jasn and Jessica, which in return, come with many nieces and nephews. Something I find pretty unique is the fact that James' siblings names are also Jessica and Jason, and my mother is a twin and so is James! I attended ALHS where I graduated in 2009. I have a family of five, which consist of my two children, fiancé James, and our dog, Charlie. We still reside in the Council Bluffs area where I am currently a stay-at-home mom and waitress part-time, while my fiancé James is a teacher and soccer coach at Lewis Central High School.

As a mother, daughter, fiancé, friend and sister I wear many hats. In my spare time, I like to cheer on my favorite sports teams, I like to be in touch with my spiritual side, I like to get outside, I enjoy crafting things and I am very fashionable. I would like to take the time below to explain some of the hats I wear, along with the hobbies I enjoy when I get the time to do so.

OUTDOORSY

I’m a Hawkeyes, Vikings, and Laker fan, however I don’t keep up with sports as much as I should. I am more of a cheerleader, to be there supporting the team through hoorays, and wearing all the gear. And it kind of comes as a default when your significant other is a huge sports fan.

SPIRITUAL

I consider myself an empath, and someone who relates to people more on a soul level. I find I enjoy things that bring you back to the mind, body, and soul through natural healing and positive thoughts. I have a psychic/medium I follow frequently, that I really admire, as she emits a positive message, while still telling you the truth you need to hear.

I hope to one day make more out of this hobby, than just simply crafting.
